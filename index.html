Profile

Hi, I'm Oliver.

I'm a self-taught front-end developer based in London.

Passionate about creating user-friendly and stylish web projects, I am also a self-taught designer.

For web apps and websites I use Javascript and React.

For styling I use CSS (with some Bootstrap experience).

And for my designing I use Figma.


I consider myself to be an excellent communicator, which helps me produce exactly what the client wants as well as giving them a clear understanding of what can be achieved.

I am also critical and analytical, spotting errors and fine tuning UI/UX for the best aesthetic and practical output on projects.
